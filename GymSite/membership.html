Membership Options

Explore our range of membership plans tailored to fit your lifestyle.

Basic Membership - "Fitness Starter"

Embark on your fitness journey with our Basic Membership plan. Ideal for beginners, this plan offers unlimited access to gym equipment and a selection of group exercise classes each month. You'll receive a complimentary fitness consultation to help set your goals and start on the right track. Enjoy the freedom to explore different workout styles at your own pace.

Premium Membership - "Total Health Advocate"

Our Premium Membership is designed for those who take their health seriously. Along with all the benefits of the Basic Membership, you'll get additional perks like unlimited classes, free guest passes, and access to premium equipment. Personal training sessions are included monthly, ensuring your routine is optimized for maximum results. Plus, get discounts on our health shop products and special events.

Elite Membership - "Ultimate Wellness Warrior"

The Elite Membership is the pinnacle of personalized fitness and well-being. You'll enjoy all the benefits of the Premium Membership, plus exclusive access to our wellness spa and relaxation area. Take advantage of one-on-one sessions with our top trainers and nutritionists, specialized workout programs, and comprehensive health evaluations. As an Elite member, you'll have priority booking for all classes and events, as well as access to VIP gym amenities.

Benefits of Joining

  • Access to all facilities
  • One free personal training session per month
  • Discount on all products and services
